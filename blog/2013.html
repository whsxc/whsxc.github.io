Summer Fun

Wednesday, May 22nd, 2013

Summer is upon us!

As all cross country coaches and veteran runners can attest, the fall season will be made or broken by what you do between now and August 12. Put in some great mileage over the next three months and you can expect a great fall season. Slack of for the next three months and, well, you can expect your results to be subpar.

Starting Tuesday, May 28th we will meet at 7am at the Widefield High School track to run. On Tuesdays and Thursdays we will lift weights after running.

As important as running over the summer is, we don't want the summer to be all drudgery and no fun. We will kick off the pure fun part of the summer with a team dinner at Coach Nelson's house on May 30th from 6-8 pm. You can get directions at any of our first three summer runs.

Many other fun events will follow. Stay tuned for a complete list of fun events and important summer dates that will be coming soon.

WHS Cross Country Invite

Sunday, September 8th, 2013

Bringing home 12 individual awards and a team trophy, we represented Widefield very well at our home meet. With temperatures in the 90s, the conditions were far from ideal. Continuing the trend established at the Cheyenne Mountain Stampede, this year's version of WHSXC continued to run hard, compete, and persevere in tough conditions rather than folding in challenging circumstances.

This Tuesday brings the Florence Husky Invitational and a forecast calling for temperatures in the low 70s and 50% chance of showers. As long as lightening stays away, the favorable conditions could give us a great chance to see what we can really do!

Be sure to talk to your teachers about missing 7th hour on Tuesday!
