Widefield High School Cross Country Invitational

Widefield High School is excited to be hosting its 14th annual cross country meet this year! This is the place for all information pertaining to our meet!

This year we are running our newer course and having a late afternoon/early evening start time. The course is mostly grass and packed trail with a few short stretches of asphalt. If you have run our meet before, this course should be much faster than the old course.

Date

Friday, September 3rd

Time

5:30pm
All Girls
6:05pm
All Boys
Location ---- Widefield High School
615 Widefield Dr
Colorado Springs, CO 80911
Please note: spectators and busses are asked to park in different places. Please follow the appropriate directions!
Directions to Race Site Course ---- The course is almost entirely grass and dirt trails with very short areas of pavement and concrete. It starts and finishes at the Widefield High School stadium area with most of the course at the Widefield Parks and Recreation grounds. Awards ---- The top finishers in each Varsity race will receive awards. The top 2 teams in each Varsity race will receive a plaque. Results ---- After the meet has finished a link to the results will be posted on Milesplit.
