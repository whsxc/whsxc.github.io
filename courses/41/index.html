Directions

Clement Park, 80123

We have never been to this course before, so these directions are from online maps. However, there is additional information after the directions. Take I-25 north to CO-470. Go west on CO-470. Exit on Wadsworth and head north. Turn right onto Bowles. The park will be on the right. Clement Park has limited parking for spectators as most of the parking will be occupied by buses. Additional parking will be available at Columbine High School which is located just south of the park. Columbine High School is located at 6201 S. Pierce Street, 80123.
