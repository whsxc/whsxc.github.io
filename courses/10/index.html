Englewood High School

Course Description | Directions | Top Runs | Meets

Course Description

A fast course due to the very hard surfaces, this race can be fast. It also can be rough on shins. It is not nearly as flat as one would expect for the times it produces, but the uphills are all gradual and followed by nice downhills. It is essentially three 1 mile loops with a variation at the start and finish.

Directions

3800 S. Logan Street, 80113

I-25 north to Hampden (Hwy 285). West on Hampden to Logan. Go left on Logan for 1.5 blocks. The school is on the east side of Logan.
