TCA @ Fountain Regional Park

Course Description | Directions | Top Runs | Meets

Course Description

A start and finish on often wet, long grass are the only aspects of this course that even hint at slow. Once down on the packed dirt trail, the runners go around a nearly 1 mile loop twice before heading back to the start/finish area. The only major elevation changes happen as the runners go from the park and start/finish area down onto the trail and when they go back again.

Directions

Take I-25 to Exit 132. Go east on Mesa Ridge Parkway and take the first exit to US 85/87. Go 0.9 miles south to the Duckwood entrance to the park (the area with the soccer fields).
