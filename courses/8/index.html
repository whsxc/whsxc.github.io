Directions

Take I-25 south to Highway 50 West. Take Hwy. 50 West to Pueblo Boulevard. Turn left (south) on Pueblo Boulevard. Go over the Arkansas River to the next stoplight. Turn left into City Park on Goodnight. Veer to the right around the zoo and to the golf course.
