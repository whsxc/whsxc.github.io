Pathfinder Park

Course Description | Directions | Top Runs | Meets

Course Description

Flat with little or no shade, this course is essentially an out and back, but not the normal kind. From the start it gets onto the river path where it makes a figure 8 around the horse stables, back onto the river path, around the lake, onto the river path heading back the way you came, around the horse stables going the opposite direction to the finish. We have always run good times, but the lack of shade combined with the typical heat we experience at the meet has traditionally made it slower than it could be.

Directions

20209 Colorado 115, 81226

Take Hwy. 115 through Florence. Stay. Go approximately 1 mile west of Florence. Pathfinder Park will be north of Highway 115.
