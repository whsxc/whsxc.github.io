Palmer Lake

Directions | Top Runs | Meets

Directions

Take I-25 to the Monument exit. Exit west and follow highway 105 through Monument and northwest through the town of Palmer Lake. As you get to the end of town, turn right on County Line Road and go across the rail road tracks. The park will be on your right. Notice: the actual lake at Palmer Lake is dried up, so look for what looks like it used to be a lake instead of an actual lake! The race starts with a loop around the "lake."
