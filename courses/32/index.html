Course Description

The course is almost entirely grass and dirt trails with very short areas of pavement and concrete. It is two loops with a variation at the beginning of the first loop and the end of the second loop. It is deceptively difficult, between the long, extended hills, the lateral running on grass hills, and the continual ups and downs.
