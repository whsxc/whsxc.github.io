Directions

From I-25 take exit 135. Turn left onto Academy Blvd. Take the Fountain Exit off Academy. Turn right (South) on Highway 85/87. Go to the first stoplight and turn left onto Main Street. Go to the 3rd traffic light and turn right on Norman Drive. Take the second left onto Widefield Dr. Turn right on the next street (Norman Dr.). Stay on Norman to the parking lot by the stadium.
