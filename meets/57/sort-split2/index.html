Team highlights

-The top four boys were separated by only 27 seconds. The top 5 boys were only separated by 44 seconds!
-The top four girls were separated by only 25 seconds!
-The boys team placed 14th out of 31 schools. Almost half of the 4A teams in the state ran in the meet.
-Six runners (Audra, Karisa, Kat, Anthony, Robby, and Connor) completed their first high school cross country race!
-Luke Wall and Jessica Shoop were the top finishers on the team, a first for each of them.
-Luke Wall and Ben Gallogly both set new PR's. Ben broke his old PR by 2:27! Luke set his new PR by 8 seconds. David Powers was within 17 seconds of his PR, Sean Zimkas was within 4 seconds of his PR, and Nick Wilson was within 2 seconds of his PR!
