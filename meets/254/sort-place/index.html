In the girls race, the lead bike led the girls the wrong way to the finish resulting in the girls running about .1 miles too short. Using these results on this website would impact all future PRs, top 10 lists, etc. So, we are putting those times below. The times listed in the results above are adjusted by taking the time each girl ran, dividing it into the distance they ran, and adding another .1 miles at the same pace. All places and mile 1 and mile 2 times listed above are accurate as they were not affected by the change in course.
