Team highlights

It was the third year in a row our boys won both the individual and team titles!

Max Martinez set the course record by 1 second.

The JV races saw much hotter conditions than the varsity races as the morning heated up rapidly. Times for the JV, and particularly the girls JV, were certainly affected by this.
