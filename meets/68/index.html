Team highlights

Team Highlights
-As a team, we recorded 8 PCRs, 3 PRs, and 1 Season Best!
-The boys team finished 4th out of 11 teams. Three of the teams in the boys race were ranked in the top 7 going into the meet. They boys beat all of the teams from our Region who were in attendance.
-The girls team finished 5th out of 11 teams. They beat all teams from our Region.
-Anthony became the 9th member of the team to break 20:00 this season. He ran his 4th consecutive PR.
-The top 5 girls were separated by only 30 seconds! The top 5 boys were only 54 seconds apart.

Athletes of the Week:
Boys: Anthony Erker
Anthony is on an incredible hot streak! He has run a PR in each of his last 4 races, dropping his time from 22:02 to 19:52 in that span. This week he ran back to back PRs only 2 days apart!

Girls: Taylor Anderson
Taylor ran her 3rd PR in a row and finished 3rd on the team, her highest team place ever! Her 37th place finish is her highest finish ever as she is running her best at the right time!
