Team highlights

+ Twenty six runners completed the 5k course.Of those, 4 ran their first ever high school 5k race!
+ The girls finished 5th out of 10+ teams.The boys finished 6th as a team.

-As a team, we recorded 10 Personal Course Records and 3 season bests.PCRs over 60 seconds were:Paul (1:00), Jack (1:28), Michael (2:51), Kimberly (1:31), Sina (1:49), and Brittni (3:58)

-The 2nd thru 4th boys were separated by only 25 seconds, the 2nd thru 5th by 45 seconds, and the 6th thru 13th boys were separated by only 65 seconds!

-In total, we had 12 runners earn points for finishing in the top half of their race.The top 5 girls all were in the top 50% of their race!

-Tori Cunningham led the girls team in her first ever high school race.Thomas Powers finished 2nd in the boys race.
