Team highlights

Team Highlights
-The boys team finished 6th out of 18 teams. The girls team finished 11th out of 18 teams.
-As a team, we ran 4 PRs, 3 PCRs, and 3 Season Bests.
-On a course that ran slower than it did at this time last year, 5 runners still ran better times than they did at the same race last year.

Athletes of the Week:
Boys: Nick Wilson
At the Cougar Classic, Nick ran a breakthrough race, breaking the 20:00 barrier with room to spare. Two days later, on what proved to be a slower than usual Fountain Course, Nick improved upon his time, running back to back PRs and making a statement that he is into the 19’s to stay!

Girls: Audra Bergman and Grace Norris
Taking turns leading the girls team, Audra and Grace brought some much needed consistency to the girls’ team this past week. After breaking 24:00 for the second time ever at the Cougar Classic, Audra turned around and ran a solid PR at Fountain, finishing in the top half overall. Grace’s times for her two races were only 5 seconds apart as she demonstrated senior leadership and great consistency. After leading the team at the Cougar Classic, Grace turned around two days later and placed right where our girls’ pack had been in the Fountain race a year ago!
