Team highlights

-As a team we ran a total of 17 PRs, 2 PCRs and 4 Season Bests. Only one runner who was not running in their first ever meet did not record one of the above.
-The boys team finished in 9th place out of 32 teams. They were 2nd among all 4A schools and first out of teams from our region.
-The girls team finished in 17th place overall but were 5th out of 4A schools. They were 2nd among teams from our region.
-New names on the Top 10 Lists from this meet include: Audra Bergman (#2 All runs, #2 SO), Taylor Anderson (#8 All-time, #8 SO), Danielle Pacheco (#7 FR), Karisa Sabisch (#8 JR), Luke Wall (#2 All-Time, #5 JR), and Robby Giese (#9 All-time).
-We ran many big PRs as a team. PRs of more than 30 seconds were set by: Abi (2:55), Katelynn (2:02), Alex (1:53), Cassie (1:40), Charmas (1:07), Ethan (:57), Danielle (:53), Sarah (:50), Alysia (:42), Kat (:31), and Kendall (:30)
-For the first time all year, JV runners made a push for, and earned Varsity spots for the next race.
-5 boys and one girl earned letter points for their places in the meet.
